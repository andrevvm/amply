About

Muxamp is a unified playlist that lets you enjoy media from SoundCloud and YouTube in the same playlist. The site is powered by SoundCloud and YouTube.

Muxamp wants to get out of your way. To use the site, use the search bar to locate audio and video from among the following sites:

  1. SoundCloud
  2. YouTube

Double-click tracks to play. Drag and drop tracks to move them.

Copy and paste the URL to share your playlist.
